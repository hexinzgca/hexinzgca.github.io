    • A new perspective for nonadiabatic dynamics with phase space mapping models
      2019
      Journal of Chemical Physics
      Volume 151, Issue 2, 024105. This paper pioneers a novel uniformly constrained sampling method for MMST (Meyer-Miller-Stock-Thoss) (electronic) mapping variables, marking the first application of such a strategy with (nuclear) Wigner phase space in nonadiabatic dynamics. This innovation establishes a crucial foundation for the constraint phase space (CPS) for nonadiabatic systems (e.g. quantum dissipative systems). The idea significantly advanced the theoretical and practical framework of MMST mapping-based approaches.
