    🔬 项目组介绍
    贺鑫博士将于2025年加入北京中关村学院，任助理教授，并牵头组建项目制课题组。

    课题组核心研究方向为AI4S（人工智能赋能科学发现），聚焦“材料性质预测、结构靶向生成与机制过程研究”，覆盖理论基础与实际应用两大维度，具体包括：

    • 基础研究：化学与物理问题的理论研究；人工智能方法及AI4S核心框架开发。
    • 应用研究：
      • 人工智能驱动的化学/材料领域科学设计及相关应用，推动“性质预测—结构生成—机制解析”一体化研究落地；
      • 相互作用深度表征与研究：开发新型单态/多态、局域/非局域机器学习力场；
      • 基于生成模型的统计力学研究：如物理系统系综的相空间（点/路径）采样，用于解析材料的热力学与动力学性质；
      • 动力学加速技术：致力于实现多尺度（分子）动力学模拟加速，提升计算效率与精度；
      • 量子分布修正与经典-量子过渡：开发量子分布修正与优化方法，保障量子系统的精准表征与模拟，弥合宏观与微观现象间的认知鸿沟。
    • 探索性研究：量子生物学；多尺度分子模拟与建模。

    🔬 About the Research Group
    Ph.D Xin He will join Beijing Zhongguancun Academy as an Assistant Professor in 2025 and establish a project-based research group.

    The group focuses on AI4S (AI for Scientific Discovery), with core research areas including material property prediction, structure-targeted generation, and mechanism research, covering both fundamental theory and practical applications:

    • Fundamental Research: Theoretical aspects of chemical and physical problems; development of AI methods and AI4S frameworks.
    • Applied Research:
      • AI-driven scientific design and related applications in chemistry/materials science, enabling integrated research of “property prediction – structure generation – mechanism analysis”;
      • In-depth representation and research of interactions: Development of novel single-state/multi-state and local/non-local force fields;
      • Statistical mechanics based on generative models: Phase space (point/path) sampling of physical ensembles to understand the thermodynamic and kinetic properties of materials;
      • Dynamics acceleration: Focus on accelerating multi-scale (molecular) dynamics simulations to improve computational efficiency and accuracy;
      • Quantum distribution correction and classical-quantum transition: Development of quantum distribution correction and optimization methods to ensure accurate characterization and simulation of quantum systems, bridging the gap between macroscopic and microscopic phenomena.
    • Exploratory Research: Quantum biology; multi-scale molecular simulation/modeling.

    👨‍🏫 About the Principal Investigator (PI)
    Xin He holds Bachelor’s and Doctoral degrees in Chemistry from Peking University, where he studied under Professor Jian Liu (Member of the International Academy of Quantum Molecular Science). His doctoral research focused on the development of trajectory-based nonadiabatic dynamics methods based on quantum phase space formalism. Later, he conducted postdoctoral research at Tsinghua University as a Shuimu Scholar.

    His research findings have been published in leading journals, including Journal of Chemical Theory and Computation, J. Phys. Chem. Lett., Accounts of Chemical Research, and The Journal of Physical Chemistry. He has received numerous awards and scholarships, such as the Outstanding Doctoral Dissertation Award of Peking University, the President’s Scholarship, and the HORIBA Special Scholarship in Physical Chemistry.

    Ph.D Xin He’s current research focuses on developing novel theoretical frameworks and computational methods for exploring complex chemical and physical systems, while advancing the practical application of these theories and methods in the study of functional materials. He places particular emphasis on innovating research paradigms centered on AI4S (AI for Scientific Discovery).
